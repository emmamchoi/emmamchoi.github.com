Papercraft

The challenge was to make some form of craft that was both Halloween and Realm of the Mad God themed. It was for a contest, so the goal is also to place and gain some video game currency as a prize. I made a paper craft decoration of a character from the game, Totalia the Malevolent, as she is a Halloween-time boss. I utilized multiple layers of paper and foam adhesives to achieve a 3D look.
