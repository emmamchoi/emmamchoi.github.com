Trash Game

The challenge was to design a game using Processing language for DES 111 that addressed a critical topic. I addressed the issue of the growing amounts of trash/litter on our planet, and added facts through the game to hopefully engage with the viewer. I aimed to leave the player with some thoughts on the issue, hopefully that of concern.
