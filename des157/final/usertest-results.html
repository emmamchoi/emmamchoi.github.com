User Test Results

Feedback Summary

Overall, the navigation through the site seemed to be relatively intuative to most people. There isn't too much going on, so I don't think it's too overwhelming or anything. One point brought up is that the focal point is kind of the bottom bar, so your eye is drawn there first. The instructions, on a side bar, is not as in focus, which may be something you want to see first.

Some mentioned the navigation bar could have a smoother scroll effect, since my current implementation is very simple, with no smooth transitions, and has 3 locations. Smoother transitions could also be applied to hover actions.

Some suggestions for info included are links to the place, location, my drink order, and something indicating the current place you are on (aside from the side text, eg. a title or something). Additionally, the readability of the text could be improved with a greater line height.
