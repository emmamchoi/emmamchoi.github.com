Project Brief

Project Goals

In relation to the comparison projects, my own project will be much simpler. The elements related would likely be only vaguely similar, encapsulating more of a similar idea than being just like the studies. Additionally, I might make adjustments along the way if I can't figure things out as I imagine them, so we shall see.

Design Strategies

Working off my baseline, I will probably keep a relatively monochrome color scheme (now it is a blue). Since the logos I am using have a variety of colors, I figure the other colors present should be relatively tame, so there isn't too much going on. I place a figure in the center to be a kind of focal point, and the rest of the information falls around it. A slideshow/bar of images for choices is relatively typical/intuative, and works fine placed at the bottom of the screen, I believe. I also kept some symmetry with the text boxes on both sides. However, if I were to implement a text box that looks like a speech bubble (a suggestion from critiques before), I might then change both style-wise, or change the layout.

Interaction Strategies

I might consider some more movement/animations to incorporate (eg. rather than the figure in the middle being the same/stationary, there could be animations to switch through different poses). The scroll bar is interactive, and there is a bit of narrative interaction between the viewer and the text.

Experience Goals

I hope to make a relatively simple but engaging experience. I don't want to overload information on the page, but I want to have enough to provide the user with something to read.
